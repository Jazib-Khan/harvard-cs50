Binary:

- Binary = base 2 numeral system.
- Information in machines and technology are represented by off an on bits.
- 1 byte = 8 bits

ASCII:

- Letters are represented by an assigned number (A = 65), This is known as ASCII.

Unicode:

- Characters are represented by Unicode which is a superset of ASCII.
- Unicode is a base 16 system otherwise as hexadecimal.

Colour:

- Red, Green and Blue each represented by an assigned number of a byte.
